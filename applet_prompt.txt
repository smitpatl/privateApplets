Imagine you have a small bookshelf and start with 5 books. Every month, you add 3 new books to your collection. How many books will you have after 6 months?

Solution:

Start with the initial number of books:

Initially, you have 5 books.
Add 3 books each month:

Month 1: (5 + 3 = 8)
Month 2: (8 + 3 = 11)
Month 3: (11 + 3 = 14)
Month 4: (14 + 3 = 17)
Month 5: (17 + 3 = 20)
Month 6: (20 + 3 = 23)
After 6 months, you will have 23 books.
